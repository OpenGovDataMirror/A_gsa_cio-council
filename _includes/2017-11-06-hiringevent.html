ACCESSIBILITY & REASONABLE ACCOMODATION:

The CIO Council is committed to ensuring all attendees have equal access to the Federal Tech and Cyber Hiring and Recruitment Event. The venue provides elevators and ramps for those who need them.

Interpreting services and assistance for the blind or visually impaired will be available for participants. These services must be pre-requested by October 23, 2017. To request assistance, please email feedback@cio.gov with the subject line “Reasonable Accommodation Request” and indicate the type of accommodation needed.
